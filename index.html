  • Water Efficiency: Uses up to 90% less water than conventional farming.
  • Space-Saving: Grows more in less space, perfect for urban settings.
  • Pesticide-Free: Eliminates the need for harmful pesticides and herbicides.
  • Year-Round Growing: Independent of seasons, ensuring a constant supply of fresh produce.
